Objectives
A range of topics related to geometric representation and computation. We will study fundamental data structures and algorithms from computational geometry and their applications to problems that occur in practice.
 
Topics include (not complete):
  • Introduction and convex hulls
  • Line segment intersection
  • Triangulating a polygon
  • Casting
  • Planar point location
  • Voronoi diagrams
  • Delaunay Triangulations
  • Robot Motion Planning
  • Medial Axis
